About Me

My name is Justin Steele. I was born and raised in Sylvania Ohio, a suburban community outside the city of Toledo. I grew up playing organized sports: Baseball, basketball, football and a couple years of soccer. I loved sports. Team sports taught me valuable lessons like team work, listening, leading and how to set goals and develope the skills necessary to achieve these goals which has transferred over into my adult life and career. In highschool I wrestled and played two years of football. I also joined the competition cheer-leading team my senior year which was the same time as wrestling season so balance the two was a challenge.

I have always been a fan of snowboarding. In high school I started getting into snowboarding heavily and going out to ride at the closest "mountains" I could find which for me where up in Michigan at Alpine Valley or Mt Brighton. Both are pretty small but they build parks and pipes pretty well. My first experience on a "real" mountain was out here in Colorado at Loveland. We rode a half day before heading up to Steamboat springs for a weekend trip with friends. Steamboat quickly became my favorite place to ride with their champaign powder and sick tree runs such as the christmas tree bowl. That was it for me. I came back out here to look for a job. I was invited to live my friend and his wife who had already been living here for a couple of years. I moved here at the end of March 2006 and have enjoyed every minute of it. I have an 11 year old son who started ski lessons when he was 5 and has switched back and forth between boards and ski's.
